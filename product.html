Groups

Preference based group assignment made easy

As former campers we have felt the disappointment of repeatedly not getting into our first choice of camp electives and as former staff members we understand the painstaking time and effort spent organizing these groups. Our solution offers an alternative method to this problem.

This feature allows camps to perform assignment of groups based on camper preferences. Our proprietary algorithm prioritizes campers that did not get their first choice, assuring all campers get a chance to be placed into their primary group/elective. Our intuitive interface simplifies the complex mathematics behind generating an optimal assignment into a web page that any staff member can pick up instantly. By using this software, camps can save the tremendous amount of time and effort spent on organization and instead spend it creating more memorable positive experiences for campers.
