Groups

Preference based group assignment made easy

As former campers we have felt the disappointment of repeatedly not getting into our first choice of camp electives and as former staff members we understand the painstaking time and effort spent organizing these groups. There was never an easy way to make every camper happy when it came to putting them in groups based on their preferences.

Our unique software solution utilizes cutting edge mathematics to deliver an optimal solution to the group assignment problem based on input parameters. Our intuitive interface simplifies the complex mathematics behind the algorithms we use so that any staff member can use it. Our proprietary algorithm prioritizes campers that did not get their first choice in the previous assignment, ensuring all campers get a chance to be placed into their primary group. By using our product, camps can save the tremendous amount of time and effort spent on organization and instead spend it creating more memorable positive experiences for campers.

  • Instantly assigns campers to groups based on pre-selected preferences
  • Allows setting of minimum and maximum group sizes
  • Allows for pairing or seperation of specific campers
  • Ensures campers that did not get their first choice will be prioritized in the next assignment

Docs

A program bank you'll never lose track of

In the summer of 2013, we developed a bullying program for our campers (a group of middle school boys) who were constantly tormenting each other. The program was incredibly successful and the boys really connected in a way we had never seen them do before. While we all agreed that the program was effective, we had no way of passing it on to others for future use. When we wrote the program, there was no way for us to see similar programs and look at what others had done and read their own reflections on how the program went overall. Often, when a camp even has a program bank, it is a disorganized collection of papers and word documents that are impossible to sift through to find what you are looking for.

Our programming bank models the activities in a way that makes it easy to document both old and new programs at camp so that you'll never lose track of them. Future staff members can then retrieve old programs based on keywords and learn how to run the activity with little effort. All of this happens within our user-friendly environment and all of your programs are backed up on our secure database. Each program within the database allows users to rate and comment on the program, so it's easy to give feedback that other users will be able to see. This makes activities better and easier to run so that camper's can get the most out of them.

  • Stores programs with descriptions for future use
  • Searches for programs using keywords, rating, and date created
  • Allows users to rate and comment on programs to give feedback for future users
  • Shows programs marked as 'public' to all users, so camps can work together to bring better experiences to all campers
