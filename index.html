Who We Are

Growing up as campers at Crane Lake Camp together, we have always felt a strong connection to camp as it was such a positive experience for us growing up. This connection has grown into a passion for helping camps provide the best experience possible for their campers. We hope that BunkMates can be used to keep counselors out of the office so that they can spend time creating positive memories for campers.

Andrew came up with the idea for BunkMates in the spring of 2016 while still an undergraduate at Carnegie Mellon University. He realized that a lot of the mathematics and computer science that he was learning in his classes could be applied to problems he remembered facing in his many summers as both a counselor and a camper at Crane Lake Camp. After validating his ideas with a couple hundred lines of code, Andrew decided to try to turn this idea into a reality. He contacted three of his friends from Crane Lake and the four of them would go on to create what is now known as BunkMates
