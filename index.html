Our Team

We are four former campers trying to make camps better through technology

Our mission is to provide a better camp experience for camps and campers using software solutions

Who We Are

Growing up as campers at Crane Lake Camp together, we have always felt a strong connection to camp as it was such a positive experience for us growing up. This connection has grown into a passion for helping camps provide the best experience possible for their campers. We hope that BunkMates can be used to keep counselors out of the office so that they can spend time creating positive memories for campers.

Andrew Resnikoff

Email: andrew@bunkmatesapp.com

Andrew came up with the idea for BunkMates in the spring of 2016 while still an undergraduate at Carnegie Mellon University. He realized that a lot of the mathematics and computer science that he was learning in his classes could be applied to problems he remembered facing in his many summers as both a counselor and a camper at Crane Lake Camp. After validating his ideas with a couple hundred lines of code, Andrew decided to try to turn this idea into a reality. He contacted three of his friends from Crane Lake and the four of them would go on to create what is now known as BunkMates

Jake Horowitz

Email: jake@bunkmatesapp.com

Jake is a recent graduate of the Goizueta Business School at Emory University. He spent 8 summers at Crane Lake Camp as both a camper and a tennis instructor. He loved his years at Crane Lake Camp, but was often frustrated with the amount of time spent on front office activities that could have been easily automated. When Andrew presented Jake with the idea for BunkMates, Jake immediately realized how helpful this product would be to camps across the country. His genuine interest in streamlining camp processes coupled with his business acumen will allow Jake to assist with any needs camps may need.

Jonathan Bennett

Email: jon@bunkmatesapp.com

Jonathan studied Psychology as an undergraduate at the University at Albany with future plans of helping people through social work. Jon spent 7 summers as both a camper and counselor, alongside the rest of the BunkMates team. Through BunkMates, he hopes to help improve the quality of camping for future staff and campers.

Rob Weissman

Email: rob@bunkmatesapp.com

Rob is a graduate of CUNY Baruch College who spent his summers at Crane Lake Camp with the rest of the BunkMates team. He has experience working at Crane Lake as a Programming Assistant, giving him the knowledge of what is most essential to a programming team. Prior to this, he spent a considerable amount of time in high school developing programs for his peers as the Programming Vice President of the North American Federation of Temple Youth’s New York Area Region. His vast experience on the creative end of camping and youth programming makes him able to provide the proper support to your camp.
