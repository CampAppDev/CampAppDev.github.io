GROUPS

Preference based group assignment made easy

As former campers we have felt the disappointment of repeatedly not getting into our first choice of camp electives and as former staff members we understand the painstaking time and effort spent organizing these groups. There was never an easy way to make every camper happy when it came to putting them in groups based on their preferences.

Our unique software solution utilizes cutting edge mathematics to deliver an optimal solution to the group assignment problem based on input parameters. Our intuitive interface simplifies the complex mathematics behind the algorithms we use so that any staff member can use it. Our proprietary algorithm prioritizes campers that did not get their first choice in the previous assignment, ensuring all campers get a chance to be placed into their primary group. By using our product, camps can save the tremendous amount of time and effort spent on organization and instead spend it creating more memorable positive experiences for campers.

  • Instantly assigns campers to groups based on pre-selected preferences
  • Allows setting of minimum and maximum group sizes
  • Allows for pairing or seperation of specific campers
  • Ensures campers that did not get their first choice will be prioritized in the next assignment
